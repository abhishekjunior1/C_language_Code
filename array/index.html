Solar Energy At LPU. Lovely Professional University has implemented several initiatives to promote the use of solar energy on its campus. Some of the notable ones are: Solar Panels: The university has installed a large number of solar panels on the rooftops of various buildings on its campus. These solar panels generate electricity from sunlight, which is used to power various facilities in the university such as street lights, water heaters, and more. Solar Water Heating System: LPU has also installed a solar water heating system in its hostels and other buildings. This system uses solar energy to heat water, which is then used for various purposes such as bathing and washing. Solar-Powered Vehicles: The university has also introduced solar-powered vehicles on its campus. These vehicles use solar energy to charge their batteries and are used for transportation within the university. Solar-powered Wi-Fi: LPU has also installed solar-powered Wi-Fi on its campus. This Wi-Fi system is powered by solar panels, which makes it more eco-friendly and sustainable. Overall, LPU has taken several steps to promote the use of solar energy on its campus, and these initiatives are contributing to the university's sustainability goals.



Types of subsidies


Capital Subsidy This subsidy is provided to help cover the upfront cost of solar panel installation, which includes the cost of equipment and installation charges.

Subsidy: This subsidy helps to reduce the cost of financing the installation of solar panels by reducing the interest rate charged on loans for solar panel installation.l>


ax Benefits: TThe Indian government provides several tax benefits to individuals and companies that install solar panels. These benefits include exemptions on income tax, customs duty, and excise duty.

Renewable Energy Certificates (RECs):T RECs are a type of subsidy that are issued to renewable energy generators such as solar panel owners. RECs can be traded on the open market, which provides an additional revenue stream for solar panel owners.

Accelerated Depreciation: TThis subsidy allows solar panel owners to depreciate their solar panels at an accelerated rate, which reduces their taxable income.

Renewable Purchase Obligations (RPOs): TRPOs are targets set by state electricity regulatory commissions for the purchase of renewable energy by distribution companies. RPOs create a market for renewable energy and help to promote the adoption of solar panels.
